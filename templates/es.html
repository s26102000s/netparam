Two speciliased certificate courses has been launched for Examination Management and Data Recovery domians in view od the emerging requirement of skilled manpower for online examinaition management.

Certificate Course in Online Exam Management System

Objective : This course will enable the participants to work with the Linux based operating systems and Exam & Bio‐metric tools used by CDAC to conduct online Exams managed by Centre for Development of Advanced Computing.

Prerequisite : Fundamental knowledge of Computers [Total 80 hrs. course]

  • Networking Fundamentals [24 hrs.]
  • PXE boot [08 hrs.]
  • Operating System Administration (CentOS) [24 hrs.]
  • Online Exam Management System [24 hrs.]
Certificate Course in Data Recovery, Sanitization & Loss Preventions

Objective : This course will enable the participant to learn and understand the various tool and techniques to recover the lost data of the storages disk/drives and various data sanitization techniques.

Prerequisite : Fundamental knowledge of Computers [Total 80 hrs. course]

  • Introduction to Data management and storage devices [12 hrs.]
  • Permanent data Deletion (Data Sanitization) [16 hrs.]
  • Data Loss Prevention [16 hrs.]
  • Data Destruction [18 hrs.]
  • Data Recovery [18 hrs.]
