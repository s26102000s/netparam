Education and Training

We impart education in diverse range of courses starting from digital literacy to Post graduate diploma in advanced computing (PGDAC) with wide range of Summer Internship, Certificate and Corporate courses in emerging technologies. We also offer various capacity building programmes, skill development & vocational courses in Electrical, Electronics, Finance domains. Many of these courses are sponsored by Bihar Skill Development Mission and National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) under Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship. These learning programs create opportunities for development of stakeholders to acquire, share, and apply global and local knowledge and experiences. There are two ways of imparting education- one which is methodical and conservative and the other which is flexible and trains itself through time. With a rich experience of over a decade in this field, we have fine-tuned our education methodology to cater to all kinds of entrants in the institute by making use of the best features from both the aforementioned techniques.

Admission

Our Institute offers several courses starting from Digital Literacy to Post Graduate Diploma in Advanced Computing. Admissions to all PG Diploma courses of C-DAC are done through C-DAC's computerized Common Admission Test (C-CAT). Every year, C-CAT is usually conducted in June (for August admissions) and December (for February admissions).

How to register for C-CAT

Admissions to all PG Diploma courses of C-DAC are done through C-DAC's computerized Common Admission Test (C-CAT). Every year, C-CAT is usually conducted in June (for August admissions) and December (for February admissions).

Registration for sponsored courses

Admissions in all sponsored courses are done through C-DAC entrance test based on the eligibility, domicile and financial guideline defined by C-DAC and advertisement published in local news dailies. Monthly stipend is applicable in few of the courses as per sponsoring department guidelines. C-DAC's computerized Common Admission Test is organized locally in frequent interval for admissions.

How to register for Campus Connect Program

Admissions in Campus Connect Programme (Summer/Winter Internships) are generally taken directly on the recommendation of the college/institution as per the AICTE norms and guidelines. Referral letter in some cased are issued by the esteemed institution defining the tentative time schedule.

Campus Life

Learnings

There are two ways of imparting education- one which is methodical and conservative and the other which is flexible and trains itself through time. With a rich experience of over a decade in this field, we have fine-tuned our education methodology to cater to all kinds of entrants in the institute by making use of the best features from both the aforementioned techniques. Teaching methodology at Astric Solutions dwells upon providing conceptual skills, diffused with theoretical background and practical skills leading to perfect implementation in real life situations. Qualified & experienced staff along with a healthy student - teacher relationship goes a long way in inculcating the desire to learn more and more within the students. Through the curriculum, we have a balanced mixture of lecture, practical, tutorial & assignments and at the end a real life project design. As & when required a panel of visiting faculty provide their expert guidance on specialized & industry-oriented subjects.

Lectures

Being the tried-and-tested mode of instruction, a strong emphasis is given in the lectures for the in-depth coverage of entire syllabus by taking care of the different analytical-abilities of the students. Lectures are so designed and delivered that the students understand the practical implementation of a technology and its applications across industry. Thus Astric Solution grooms individuals with strong conceptual knowledge, and the right kind of attitude that helps the Astric Solution students to stand tall in the job market.

Practical

Practical implementation of the theoretical knowledge is of utmost importance, especially in the IT industry. With specialized infrastructure and competent faculty, we help to build intelligent software engineers out of students who can understand the concept in an effective and efficient manner by practically exploring the system. Expert lab faculties are there to assist them with well structured assignments to canalize the practical and seminars are conducted to enhance their knowledge about the system.

Tutorial

Tutorials are conducted to help the students deal with final details of topics & to prepare the student gradually to handle the problems at examination levels.

Project

After going through the entire syllabus the students are assigned with one or two projects where they can build up their skills in one particular application & put their knowledge to test. This prepares the students to handle real life situation, and at the same time, caters to their needs & thus sharpen their skill at S/W development. Every student has to develop his/her project separately, which is a part of the syllabus and after completing the project he/she will be eligible for his/her respective certification.

Presentation & Seminars

To keep up with the latest trends of the industry, seminars & presentation are organized which enhances both their knowledge & oratory skills. Regular presentations help them to become both knowledgeable individuals and persuasive speakers.

Personality Development

Students are encouraged actively to participate in extra curricular activity organized on Saturday to enhance personality traits. Visiting faculty and member of leading organization are invited to express their view and ideas about the present trends and how to achieve success and what should be taken care during the tough time.

Cultural Programs

Cultural programs are designed to both educate and celebrate. The activities begin with celebration featuring music, dance, food, games, and student organization booths. Providing the climactic finale at the end of the week is Culture Night, the annual talent showcase featuring student, local, and professional groups. Combining traditional and contemporary styles, the performances reflect the changing face of our culture with ideas and thoughts of the students. These events would not be possible without the hard work and dedication of the students and the faculty.

Sports Activities

To bring team sprite among the students, the institute also organize different indoor and outdoor games like cricket, football, badminton, etc. both for girls and boys and winners are awarded to honour their enthusiasm and winning attitude.

Placement Assistance

The placement cell keeps in touch with industry, alumni & students to place the right students at the right place thus helping them guide through their career dreams. Institute has its own placement cell. Pre-placement workshops are organized at the end of the session. The institute has its own strong connections with prospective Employers as well as with placement consultants. We are in touch with various placement consultants of Delhi, Kolkata, Bangalore and other cities.
